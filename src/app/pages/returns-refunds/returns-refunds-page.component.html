Overview

At rimansan, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not completely satisfied, we offer a straightforward returns and refunds process. This page outlines our policies regarding returns, refunds, and exchanges to help you shop with confidence.

Return Policy

Items purchased from rimansan may be returned within 14 days of the original shipment date, provided they are in their original condition, unused, and with all original packaging and tags intact. Returns that do not meet these criteria may not be eligible for a refund or exchange.

  • Eligibility: Products must be unused, in the same condition as received, and in their original packaging.
  • Non-returnable Items: Certain items, such as personalized products, gift cards, or items marked as final sale, are non-returnable.
  • Proof of Purchase: A valid receipt or order confirmation is required to process returns.

How to Return an Item

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at to request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
  2. Securely package the item(s) in their original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
  3. Include the RMA number and a copy of your receipt or order confirmation in the package.
  4. Ship the package to the address provided by our customer service team. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or was shipped in error.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you via email about the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original form of payment within 5-10 business days.
  • Shipping and handling fees from the original purchase are non-refundable.
  • Refunds for items purchased via PayPal or credit card will be credited to the respective account used for the purchase.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product, please contact our customer service team to confirm availability. Exchanges are subject to the same 14-day return window and condition requirements as refunds. Customers are responsible for shipping costs for exchanges unless the item is defective or was shipped in error.

Defective or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of receipt at . We will provide a prepaid return label for defective or incorrect items and offer a full refund or replacement, based on your preference and product availability.

Shipping Information for Returns

1. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect item.
2. We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure your return is delivered to us.
3. Refunds or exchanges will be processed only after the returned items are received and inspected at our facility.
4. Please allow 1-2 weeks for the entire return process, depending on shipping times and inspection.
