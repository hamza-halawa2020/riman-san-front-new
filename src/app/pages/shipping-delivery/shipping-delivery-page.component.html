Overview

At rimansan, we are committed to delivering your orders promptly and securely. This page provides detailed information about our shipping methods, delivery times, costs, and policies to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

Shipping Methods and Delivery Times

We offer a variety of shipping options to meet your needs. All orders are processed and shipped from our warehouse within 1-2 business days, unless otherwise stated. Below are our available shipping methods:

  • Standard Ground Shipping: Complimentary for all orders, delivered within 1-7 business days.
  • Next-Day Delivery: Available for an additional fee, with delivery on the next business day for orders placed before 2:00 PM EST.
  • Express Delivery: Delivered within 2-3 business days for an additional fee.
  • In-Store Collection: Available at select rimansan locations within 1-7 business days. You will be notified when your order is ready for pickup.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs vary based on the selected method and destination:

  • Standard Ground Shipping: Free for all orders.
  • Next-Day Delivery: Calculated at checkout based on location and order weight.
  • Express Delivery: Calculated at checkout based on location and order weight.
  • International Shipping: Available to select countries. Costs and delivery times are calculated at checkout based on destination.

Please refer to our Delivery FAQs for detailed information on shipping rates and restrictions.

Packaging

All purchases from rimansan are delivered in our signature orange box tied with a Bolduc ribbon, with the exception of certain oversized or promotional items. If you require special packaging or gift wrapping, please contact our customer service team at before placing your order.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your rimansan account and visiting the "Order History" section.

International Shipping

We offer international shipping to select countries. Please note:

  • Delivery times vary depending on the destination and customs processing.
  • Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by the destination country.
  • Some products may not be available for international shipping due to restrictions or regulations.

Delivery Issues

If your order is delayed, damaged, or lost during transit, please contact our customer service team at or call 1-800-XXX-XXXX (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST). We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue promptly.

Payment Methods for Shipping

rimansan accepts the following payment methods for orders, including shipping costs:

  • Credit Card: Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, JCB, Visa Electron. The total, including shipping, will be charged to your card when the order is shipped.
  • Fast Checkout: Save your credit card details securely for faster future purchases.
  • PayPal: Shop easily without entering credit card details. Your account will be charged once the order is completed. Visit paypal.com to register for a PayPal account.
